💡 The Perfect 15-Min Call Flow (Timeline)

Use this general structure for every call so you never run out of things to say or get stuck:

  • Minutes 0-2 (Ice Breaker): "Hi! Where are you joining from?" / "I saw your post about X, how is that going?"
  • Minutes 2-5 (The Pitch & Deck): "Let me quickly share my screen to show you what we are building." (Present the Ceylon Cash pitch deck briefly and confidently. Highlight the Ceypay.io launch).
  • Minutes 5-6 (The Bridge): "So that's what we do. But the main reason I reached out today is actually for networking and learning."
  • Minutes 6-12 (The Ask & Listening): Ask them your "Safe Asks" (e.g., "How does your company usually work with startups?"). Let them talk! Listen, nod, and say "That makes sense."
  • Minutes 12-15 (Wrap Up): "This was super helpful. Let's definitely stay in touch. I'll drop you an email/Telegram message right after this."
